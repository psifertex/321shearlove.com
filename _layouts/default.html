Who am I

321shearlove perfectly describes Stephanie Troyan. What she creates with shears and hair is an act of love for her craft, and for each of her client-friends. Stephanie has always wanted to help people feel good about themselves. With a Bachelor’s of Science in Psychology it helps her better understand how to communicate with her guest.

It wasn’t until helping a friend with her hair that she discovered the “shear love” of her gift, she knew she had found her calling.

Stephanie believes that with the right cut or color it will reflect The beauty that is bottled up inside each of us.

As a licensed independent stylist putting pride in her craft, she is professionally trained on all textures, lengths, colors, and cuts. She is constantly doing continued education and training to keep up with top trends. She specializes in curly textures, blonding, and only uses quality products.

When not serving guest at Lather Salon, you might find Stephanie jogging the causeway, or at the beach with her husband and three children.
